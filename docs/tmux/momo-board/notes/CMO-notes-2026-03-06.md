## Insight 5: "Sinh Loi" Positioning Rationale

Vietnamese users think in three mental buckets:
- "Gui tiet kiem" = bank deposit, locked, guaranteed, boring
- "Dau tu" = stocks, gold, risky, requires expertise
- "Sinh loi" = my money making more money — emotionally neutral, aspirational

**Product naming:** "MoMo Sinh Loi" or "Vi Sinh Loi" (Earn Wallet)

**The line:** "Tien trong MoMo cua ban dang sinh loi moi ngay" (Your money in MoMo is earning returns every day)

**Messaging rules for Phase 1:**
- Lead with the RATE: "Earn 5.8-6.2%/year, withdraw anytime"
- Show DAILY earnings: "You earned 4,521d today"
- Never show NAV fluctuation on home screen — cumulative returns only
- Compare to what they know: "Like gui tiet kiem, but withdraw anytime and earn more"
- Never say "dau tu" or "chung chi quy" in user-facing copy

**CEO approved this positioning. Going into the one-pager for Dragon Capital as a selling point.**

## Insight 6: Segment Targeting (Revised After CFO's 2.13x Ratio)

**The 2.13x ratio:** For every $1 that leaves TTT for investment, MoMo needs $2.13 of NEW external money to stay P&L neutral.

**Revised targeting hierarchy:**
1. **PRIMARY:** 935K external investors on MoMo (zero TTT cannibalization)
2. **SECONDARY:** 12M+ new-to-investing MoMo users (zero TTT risk)
3. **DO NOT TARGET:** 86,700 TTT whales (>50M VND, 0.7% of users, 60.8% of AUM) — suppression list

**TTT upgraders narrowed:** Only users with TTT balances UNDER 5M VND. Above that, migration hurts P&L.

**Pitch to Dragon Capital:** "Our economic model REQUIRES us to bring in new money, not redistribute existing deposits. Your interests and ours are perfectly aligned."

## Insight 7: Brand Architecture — TTT vs Investment Coexistence

**Problem:** TTT = safe, guaranteed. Investment = risk, volatility. Opposite emotions in one app.

**Solution: Architectural separation, not messaging.**
- TTT stays in its own sanctuary. Same screen, same green color, same "guaranteed" language. Nothing changes.
- Investment lives in a separate "Sinh Loi" tab. Different color palette (blue = growth). Different entry point.
- Never cross-contaminate home screen. Portfolio losses never appear next to TTT balance.
- TTT users who haven't opted into investment see NO investment UI. Period. Opt-in gated with education module.

**The leaky bucket test:** If TTT outflows spike >5% above baseline in first 6 months, pause and reassess.
