## Fee Structure — How It Works

Fund management fees have two components:
1. **Management Fee** — charged by fund house to investors (deducted from NAV daily)
2. **Distribution Fee (Trail Commission)** — fund house pays distributor for bringing/retaining investors

### Vietnam Open-End Fund Fee Benchmarks

| Product Type | Total Mgmt Fee | Typical Trail to Distributor |
|---|---|---|
| Money Market Fund | 0.4-0.6% of AUM | 15-25 bps (0.15-0.25%) |
| Short-term Bond Fund | 0.6-0.8% of AUM | 25-40 bps |
| Balanced Fund | 1.0-1.5% of AUM | 40-60 bps |
| Equity Fund | 1.5-2.0% of AUM | 50-80 bps |
