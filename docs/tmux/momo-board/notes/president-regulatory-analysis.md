## 1. CV Securities License Scope Analysis

**License:** No. 26/GPDC-UBCK (amended June 5, 2025)

**Current scope:**
1. Moi gioi chung khoan (Securities brokerage)
2. Tu doanh chung khoan (Proprietary trading) — added June 2025
3. Tu van dau tu chung khoan (Investment advisory)

**Does NOT explicitly list:** Phan phoi chung chi quy (fund certificate distribution)

### Legal Analysis

Under Securities Law 2019 (Luat Chung Khoan 54/2019/QH14), Article 4, Clause 1: fund certificates (chung chi quy mo) ARE classified as securities (chung khoan).

"Moi gioi chung khoan" — securities brokerage — by legal definition covers the intermediation of securities transactions, which INCLUDES fund certificates.

**However:** Circular 98/2020/TT-BTC on open-ended fund management has SPECIFIC requirements for fund distribution agents (dai ly phan phoi):
- Distribution agent must be registered with each specific fund management company
- Notification filing to SSC required
- This is an **administrative registration**, NOT a separate license
- Timeline: 2-4 weeks per fund company

**Conclusion:** CVS brokerage license provides the legal CAPACITY to distribute fund certificates. The remaining step is administrative registration as distribution agent — not a new license.

## 2. VNSC/Finhay Precedent

VNSC (formerly Finhay) distributes VCBF fund certificates on their platform. They operate on a **brokerage license** and distribute funds through **distribution agent registration** — not a separate fund distribution license.

**Implication:** Their structure mirrors what CVS would use. This is a proven regulatory pathway in Vietnam.

### Why the 2022 Partnership Hasn't Generated Volume

Three probable causes (pattern seen 3x in financial services):

1. **Buried UX** — Fund purchase added as feature checkbox, not a product experience. Users couldn't find it or didn't understand it.
2. **No education layer** — Can't drop a fund product into a payments app and expect adoption. Users need to understand what a money market fund IS.
3. **No incentive alignment** — Standard trail commission but no active marketing from MoMo. Neither side had urgency. Became shelfware.
