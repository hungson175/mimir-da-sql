Why TTT Protection Is Non-Negotiable

TTT (Tui Tiet Kiem) holds 12.46T VND ($490M) from 2.88M funded users. It generates $11.5M/year in spread revenue (2.35% net). This took 14 years of trust to build. If users see their TTT balance decline because they moved money to investment — and that investment dips even temporarily — trust erodes. The moment a user sees red where green used to be, we've damaged something money can't fix.

Whale Concentration Risk

86,700 users (0.7% of TTT) hold 60.8% of AUM. Each whale migration costs $63/year in lost spread. If just 10% of whales migrate, that's $545K/year in lost revenue. This is why whales are on the suppression list — no Sinh Lời targeting, ever.

Guardrail Philosophy

Never block users. Blocking creates resentment and pushes users to competitors. Instead: monitor, alert, and offer better alternatives. If a whale wants to move money, give them a VIP experience in TTT (higher rate, dedicated support) rather than a wall.

What It Looks Like in Practice

Dashboard shows TTT spread revenue baseline established pre-launch. If monthly spread drops below $47.9K/month below baseline, the fire alarm triggers automatically. This is not a manual review — it's an automated alert that goes to CEO and CFO simultaneously. Response within 24 hours required.

4-Step Graduated Throttle

Each step requires explicit escalation. Steps 3-4 require CEO + CFO joint approval.

1
Remove Default Prompts
Stop showing Sinh Lời suggestions to TTT users on home screen. Keep tab accessible but don't push. Reduces casual migration.
2
Add Friction
TTT-to-Sinh-Loi transfers require explicit confirmation + 24hr cooling period. "Are you sure? Your TTT earns guaranteed 3.65%." Educational interstitial.
3
Cap Migration Volume
Daily/weekly cap on TTT-to-Sinh-Loi transfers. New external money unrestricted. Requires CEO + CFO joint approval to activate.
4
Pause Sinh Lời Growth
No new Sinh Lời acquisitions from TTT base. External investor acquisition continues. Full board review within 48 hours. CEO + CFO joint approval required.

The Net Positive Scenario

If a user adds 50M VND to investment while keeping 30M VND in TTT (moving only 20M from TTT), the ratio is 50M / 20M = 2.5x — above the 2.13x threshold. Net result: +$2.19/year per user. The wallet GROWS.

Strategy: Track Total AUM, Not Just TTT

The key metric is total wallet size (TTT + Sinh Lời), not just TTT alone. If a user's total financial relationship with MoMo grows — even if TTT shrinks slightly — we're winning. The dashboard must show net financial wallet per user, not just TTT AUM.

Premium Service for Whales

Tier 3 users (>50M VND) who show interest in investing get a premium service experience: dedicated support, higher TTT rates, first access to new products. The goal is retention through better value, not restriction through walls. "Does this make users stay, or just prevent them from leaving?"
