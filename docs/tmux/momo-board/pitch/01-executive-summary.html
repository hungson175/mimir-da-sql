The Gap

Vietnam has 100 million people but only 423,000 retail fund holders. That's 0.4% penetration — the lowest in ASEAN. Meanwhile, 935,000 MoMo users are already tagged as investors, moving $170M per month through external platforms. They trust us with payments. They trust us with savings. For investment, they go elsewhere — because we gave them nothing.

The Threat

Grab Financial Group is 12-18 months from launching investment products in Vietnam. Finhay (VNSC) already distributes DCVFM funds. Every month we wait, our 935K investor base gets deeper hooks into competitor platforms. TTT's $490M AUM is safe today — but a competing savings+investment product could change that.

The Opportunity

MoMo already signed a fund distribution partnership with Dragon Capital (DCVFM) in June 2022 — the first Vietnamese super-app to offer fund trading. It became shelfware due to buried UX, zero marketing, and wrong language. The rails exist. The legal structure exists. This is not "build from scratch." This is "reactivate, rebrand, and scale."

Key Discovery: MoMo-DCVFM Partnership (June 2022)

MoMo signed a distribution deal with DCVFM in June 2022 — the first Vietnamese super-app to offer open-ended fund trading. It became shelfware because of:

1. Buried UX — one of 50+ services, no dedicated entry
2. Zero marketing — one PR article, no sustained push
3. Wrong language — "chứng chỉ quỹ," "đầu tư" jargon instead of user language

Critical Correction: DCDS Product

DCDS (DC Dynamic Stock) was initially misidentified as a money market fund. It is actually an open-ended equity fund (+32% YTD, VND 6.3T AUM). Phase 1 corrected to DCIP (short-term bonds) + DCBF (bond fund). Caught before reaching President's desk.
