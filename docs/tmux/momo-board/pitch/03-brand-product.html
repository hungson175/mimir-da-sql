The Language Decision

Vietnamese users think in three mental buckets. The word we choose determines which bucket — and which emotional reaction — we trigger:

Rejected
"Đầu tư"
Investment
Scares users. Implies expertise needed, risk, stocks. Mental model: "Tôi có thể mất tiền."
Rejected
"Tiết kiệm"
Savings
Misleads. Implies guaranteed returns like bank deposits. Regulatory risk if returns aren't guaranteed.
Chosen
"Sinh lời"
Earn returns
Emotionally neutral, aspirational, accurate. "Tiền của tôi đang sinh lời." No expertise implied.

Architectural Separation, Not Messaging

TTT = safe, guaranteed. Investment = risk, volatility. Opposite emotions in one app. The solution isn't messaging — it's architecture. They must never touch each other visually.

Why This Ordering Matters

The 2.13x offset ratio means MoMo needs $2.13 of new money for every $1 leaving TTT. By targeting external investors first, we bring pure new AUM — zero cannibalization. The suppression list protects the whale concentration that generates 60.8% of TTT revenue.

Product Ladder Logic

Start with what feels like gửi tiết kiệm (bank deposit) — guaranteed-feeling, low risk, instant liquidity. Build trust. Then offer higher-return products as users gain confidence. Never start with equity. The ladder mirrors how Vietnamese people actually learn to invest: savings → bonds → stocks.
