June 2022: The Partnership That Became Shelfware

MoMo (M_Service) signed a distribution partnership with DCVFM in June 2022. MoMo became the first Vietnamese super-app to offer open-ended fund certificate trading. Investors could open fund trading accounts in under 1 minute.

Luong Thi My Hanh (DCVFM Head of Domestic Asset Management) called it "a breakthrough in Vietnam's financial industry."

2022 — What Went Wrong

  • Buried UX — one of 50+ services, no dedicated entry
  • Zero marketing — single PR article, no campaign
  • Wrong language — "chứng chỉ quỹ," "đầu tư" jargon
  • No incentive alignment — standard trail, no urgency
  • Result: became shelfware

2026 — What's Different

  • Dedicated UX — "Sinh Lời" tab, blue palette, opt-in
  • Full marketing push — 935K investor targeting
  • User language — "sinh lời," rates, daily earnings
  • C-suite ownership — CEO, CMO, CFO aligned
  • $1.8M Year 1 commitment, kill gates defined
