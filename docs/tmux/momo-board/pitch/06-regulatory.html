President Thuan's Legal Read

Under Securities Law 2019 (Luật Chứng Khoán 54/2019/QH14):

Article 4, Clause 1Fund certificates (chứng chỉ quỹ mở) ARE securities (chứng khoán)
Brokerage definitionCovers intermediation of ALL securities transactions
ImplicationCVS brokerage license = legal capacity to distribute fund certificates

Circular 98/2020/TT-BTC — The Remaining Step

Circular 98 on open-ended fund management requires distribution agent registration — this is administrative, NOT a new license:

RequirementRegister as distribution agent with each fund company
FilingNotification to SSC (State Securities Commission)
Timeline2-4 weeks per fund company
NatureAdministrative registration, not licensing
"CVS brokerage license provides the legal CAPACITY to distribute fund certificates. The remaining step is administrative registration — not a new license."
— President Do Quang Thuan, legal analysis

VNSC/Finhay Precedent

VNSC (formerly Finhay) distributes VCBF fund certificates on their platform. They operate on a brokerage license and distribute funds through distribution agent registration — not a separate fund distribution license.

Mirror Structure

VNSC's structure mirrors what CVS would use. This is a proven regulatory pathway in Vietnam. Key confirmation needed: pull VNSC's SSC filings to verify their exact structure for our external legal opinion.

Why This Matters

If VNSC (a fintech-owned securities company) can distribute fund certificates via brokerage license + distribution agent registration, then CVS (MoMo's securities company) can do the same. The regulatory path is not novel — it's been walked before. We're following, not pioneering.
