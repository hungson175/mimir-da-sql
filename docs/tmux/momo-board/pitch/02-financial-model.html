Break-even AUM Threshold

At 0.8% blended fee (trail commission), each user needs $1,200 AUM (30.6M VND) to cover their acquisition cost within Year 1. This is roughly the equivalent of 6 months' savings for a mid-tier MoMo user. The Y1 funnel projects average balance of $556 — meaning full unit economics break-even requires AUM growth over 18-24 months.

Whale Sensitivity

High-value users (100M+ VND / $3,937) lose $63/year per migrating user ($3,937 x 1.6% spread difference). These 86,700 users hold 60.8% of TTT AUM — they are on the suppression list. No Sinh Lời targeting.

TTT AUM Concentration by Tier

Offset Ratio: 2.13x

For every $1 leaving TTT, MoMo needs $2.13 of NEW external money entering investment to stay P&L neutral. At 10% TTT migration ($49M), need $104M new external AUM. 5% capture of 935K investor segment's $2B/yr external flow = $100M — achievable.

Offset Ratio Applied

The 2.13x offset ratio means if users moving $49M from TTT also bring in $104M of NEW money, the economics are neutral. The CMO's segment strategy ensures this by targeting external investors first (zero TTT risk) and suppressing whale targeting.

Wallet Expansion — Net Positive Scenario

User adds 50M VND investment + keeps 30M VND TTT (moves 20M from TTT). Ratio: 50M / 20M = 2.5x > 2.13x threshold. Net result: +$2.19/year per user — the wallet GROWS. This is the design target: grow the pie, don't slice it differently.
