# FS Department 2025 Annual Review

**Date:** 2026-03-04
**Data Sources:** BigQuery (momovn-prod.BU_FI), all 5 core FS products
**Period:** January - December 2025

## Executive Summary

The Financial Services department had a **strong 2025**, with all five core products showing growth. Highlights:

- **Paylater** was the star performer: MAU grew +45% (989K → 1.44M), the most consistent growth engine
- **Vay Nhanh** scaled massively: disbursement value nearly tripled (+169%), from 1.16T to 3.13T VND/month
- **TTT** maintained its massive base: 3.5M+ MAU, AUM grew +23% to 11.3T VND
- **CLO** quietly built momentum: disbursements 2.4x, revenue 2.4x, diversified from 100% HC to 62% HC
- **InsurTech** showed mixed signals: seasonal peaks strong but average monthly volume declined

## Key Takeaways

1. **Lending is the growth engine.** Paylater + Vay Nhanh combined disbursed ~26T VND in 2025, with both products accelerating in H2.

2. **TTT = cash cow.** 3.5M+ MAU stable base, 11.3T AUM growing 23% — generates steady spread income (~20B/month net).

3. **Vay Nhanh's June inflection.** Average loan size jumped 30%+ mid-year, suggesting credit limit expansion or new lender products.

4. **InsurTech needs strategic attention.** Outside of seasonal peaks (Jan, Dec = motorcycle renewals), monthly volume is flat at ~75K. Travel and other lines are 100% organic — zero marketing investment.

5. **CLO building quietly.** Small absolute numbers but 2.4x growth, new bank partner (SHB), diversifying from 100% Home Credit dependency.
