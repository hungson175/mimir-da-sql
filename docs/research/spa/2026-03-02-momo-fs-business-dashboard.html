Executive Summary

Key metrics and strategic overview

🚀 STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATION: AGGRESSIVE EXPANSION

MoMo's Financial Services business has achieved 7.5x growth in 2 years, transforming from a payment wallet into Vietnam's leading digital lending platform. The 2026-2027 strategy should focus on: (1) Scaling lending to 40T VND, (2) Launching AI-powered wealth management, (3) Expanding CVS trading, while managing credit risk and regulatory compliance.

💡 Key Insights

  • Lending is the new growth engine: Vay Nhanh grew 7.5x in 2 years, now 70% from repeat borrowers
  • Cross-sell flywheel working: 55.5% of Paylater users convert to Vay Nhanh

  • Credit expansion happening: Avg loan size grew 65% (9.4M → 15.5M VND)
  • Risk increasing: Credit scores declining (762→670), NPL concern
  • TTT provides stable foundation: 13M savers, 11.2T AUM, positive cash flow
  • CVS emerging opportunity: 60K traders, 2.7B fee revenue, room to grow

Strategic Initiatives Roadmap

🎯 Lending Expansion (Priority #1)

2026 Q1-Q4
  • • Launch "Vay Nhanh Plus" for high-value borrowers (30M+ loans)
  • • Expand to 5+ lender partners (add TPBank, VPBank)
  • • Implement AI-powered credit scoring (reduce NPL by 20%)
  • • Target: 40T VND disbursed in 2026

🤖 Wealth Management Launch (Priority #2)

2026 Q2-Q4
  • • Build AI-powered investment advisor (leverage Moni)
  • • Launch round-up investing (Acorns-style)
  • • Partner with 3+ fund houses for ETF distribution
  • • Target: 1M wealth users, 5T VND AUM by end 2026

📈 CVS Trading Growth (Priority #3)

2026 Q1-Q4
  • • Add margin trading, derivatives
  • • Launch social trading (copy trading)
  • • Reduce fees to compete with SSI, VNDirect
  • • Target: 150K active traders, 10T VND monthly volume

💳 Paylater Expansion

2026 Q1-Q4
  • • Launch "Paylater for Business" (SME BNPL)
  • • Expand to education, healthcare usecases
  • • Cross-sell to 70% of Paylater users
  • • Target: 2.5M MAU, 15T VND GMV

⚡ Cost Optimization

2026 Q1-Q4
  • • Reduce TTT interest expense via dynamic rates
  • • Implement AI collections (reduce NPL 20%)
  • • Automate 80% of customer service
  • • Target: 15% cost reduction per loan

🎯 2027 Vision

  • #1 digital lender in Vietnam: 60T VND annual disbursement
  • Top 3 wealth management platform: 20T VND AUM
  • Top 5 brokerage: 500K active traders
  • Profitable FS unit: 15T VND revenue, 3T VND profit
