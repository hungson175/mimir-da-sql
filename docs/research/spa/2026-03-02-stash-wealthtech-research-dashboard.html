Implementation Roadmap

Recommended Approach: "MoMo Wealth" — Embedded Advisory Platform

Don't build a standalone app. Embed wealth management features into the existing MoMo super app, leveraging the Financial Hub, Moni AI, and existing financial products. Start with fund distribution via licensed partners, layer advisory features, then build AI-driven personalization.

Phase 1: Foundation (0-6 months)

GOAL: Quick wins with existing products

  • Round-Up to TTT: Auto-invest spare change from every payment into Tui Than Tai
  • Goal-Based Savings in Moni: "Save for vacation" / "Emergency fund" with progress tracking
  • Financial Health Score: Holistic view across spending, saving, investing (unique in VN)
  • Expand Fund Distribution: More fund certs via VFM, Dragon Capital, Eastspring partners
  • Gold DCA: Auto-buy gold on schedule (weekly/monthly)
  • Unified FinHub Dashboard: Single view of TTT + Funds + Gold + Securities
KPIs: 500K round-up users, 1M goal-based savers, 50K new fund investors

Phase 2: Advisory Layer (6-18 months)

GOAL: AI-driven personalized guidance

  • Moni → Investment Advisor: Upgrade Moni AI to recommend investment products based on user profile
  • Risk Profiling: 5-question onboarding → Conservative/Moderate/Aggressive
  • Smart Portfolios: Pre-built fund portfolios for each risk level
  • Premium Advisory Sub: 29K-79K VND/month ($1-3) for advanced features
  • Financial Education Hub: Vietnamese-language investing courses integrated in Moni
  • Cashback → Investment: Convert MoMo rewards/cashback into fund cert units
KPIs: 3M advisory users, 500K invested users, 200K premium subs, 2T VND new AUM

Phase 3: Full Platform (18-36 months)

GOAL: Compete with Grab/Stash at scale

  • AI Portfolio Manager: Auto-rebalancing, tax-aware optimization
  • Retirement Planning Tools: Pension projections, social insurance integration
  • Family Investment Goals: Joint savings/investing for couples + kids (cultural fit)
  • Fixed-Income Retail Distribution: Government bonds, corporate bonds access
  • Insurance-Investment Bundles: Protect & grow packages
  • B2B MoMo Works: Corporate financial wellness (like StashWorks)
KPIs: 8-12M invested users, $4-18B AUM, $150-300M annual revenue
