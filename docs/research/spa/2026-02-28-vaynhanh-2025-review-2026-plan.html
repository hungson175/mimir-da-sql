Growth Levers for 2026

  • 1. Avg Loan Size Expansion: 2025 trend was 12M→18M. Pushing to 20-22M avg through credit limit increases adds ~15% disbursement without more users.
  • 2. New Lender Partners: MBV is nascent (10 loans in 2025). Onboarding 1-2 more lenders (e.g., FE Credit, Home Credit) diversifies and increases approval rate for rejected applicants.
  • 3. Re-loan Optimization: Re-loan is 54% of volume. Faster re-loan cycle (auto-approved top-up within 24h of liquidation) could boost frequency 20-30%.
  • 4. FM Merchant Scale: Currently 6.5K loans but avg 34M — highest value segment. Scaling merchant financing channel could add 0.5-1T/year.
  • 5. Cross-sell from Paylater: 55% of VN borrowers came from Paylater (past pattern). Increasing Paylater→VN conversion funnel from current rate adds volume.
  • 6. Seasonal Push Strategy: H2 2025 was 89% higher than H1. Plan heavier marketing/promotion spend in H1 2026 to flatten seasonality.
