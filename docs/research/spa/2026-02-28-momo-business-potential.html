Revenue vs Volume Divergence

Revenue-Volume Divergence

P2P transaction volume grew 44% (2023-2025) but revenue declined 25%. This is fee compression from free VietQR transfers via banking apps. P2P is now an infrastructure/retention layer, not a profit center. Monetize through data and cross-sell instead.

Strategic Value: Ecosystem Glue

11.3M users make TTT MoMo's largest financial product. AUM per user is small (866K VND) but the habit of daily saving keeps users in the ecosystem. This is the source-of-funds layer: TTT savings → Paylater spending → Vay Nhanh borrowing. Upgrading to wealth management (mutual funds, robo-advisory) is the next step.

Regulatory Highlights

Circular 41/2025 — E-Wallet Rules

VND 100M/month transaction cap per wallet. Biometric KYC mandatory. Single brand per provider from Jan 2026. Consolidates market around licensed players.

BNPL Regulation — Coming 2025-2026

Mandatory disclosures, late-fee caps, eligibility checks expected. Will legitimize market and eliminate undisciplined competitors. Favors MoMo.

Cross-Border QR — Expanding

Vietnam-China QR live Dec 2025. Korea, Japan in pipeline. NAPAS interoperability opens tourist payment revenue. 30,000+ merchants expected by end 2025.

Strategic Recommendations for 2026-2027

1. Scale Vay Nhanh Aggressively (with Risk Management)

HIGH PRIORITY ACT NOW

Target: 3% → 8-10% user penetration = 2.5-3M borrowers

  • Add more lender partners beyond Viet Credit/Amber/MCredit
  • Build proprietary credit scoring using Paylater + TTT + P2P data
  • Graduated products: Paylater → Vay Nhanh → Bank partner loans
  • Critical: Build AI NPL prediction before expanding (credit score at 670)

2. Build InsurTech as Next Revenue Pillar

HIGH PRIORITY BUILD 2026

Target: 500K+ policies by end 2027

  • Embed micro-insurance into flows: travel at OTA, phone at App Store, health at BNPL
  • Target 2.49M Paylater users (already comfortable with MoMo financial products)
  • Partner 3-4 carriers for white-label, risk-sharing models
  • AI-personalized offers based on transaction patterns

3. Accept P2P Revenue Decline, Monetize Data

STRATEGIC SHIFT
  • Stop fee extraction from P2P (market moved to free VietQR)
  • Use P2P data for: credit scoring, merchant patterns, product triggers
  • Focus QR on merchant acquisition (Tier-2/3 SMEs)
  • Invest in cross-border QR (Vietnam-China tourist corridor)

4. Upgrade TTT to Wealth Management Platform

MEDIUM PRIORITY

Target: AUM/user from 866K → 2-3M VND

  • Add mutual fund distribution + structured deposits
  • AI robo-advisory for personalized savings goals
  • Tui+ upsell for higher AUM + cross-product engagement

5. AI as the Strategic Moat

FOUNDATIONAL ACT NOW
  • Credit AI: Proprietary scoring on ecosystem data (PL + TTT + P2P + merchants)
  • Product AI: Right product, right moment (flight search → travel insurance)
  • Moni AI: Financial advisor chatbot (290K MAU, 479K conversations/mo)
  • Fraud AI: 375M txns/month — detection at scale
