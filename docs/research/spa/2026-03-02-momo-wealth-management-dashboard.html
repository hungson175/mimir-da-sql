Strategy & Roadmap

Recommended features, business model, and implementation phases

Proposed Features (MVP)

🤖 AI Savings Coach

Personalized tips to increase savings based on spending patterns. Comparable to Stash Money Coach.

🪙 Round-up Investing

Auto-invest spare change from transactions. Vietnam's first true round-up investing feature.

💬 Wealth Q&A

Natural language answers about finance, investing, and MoMo products. Extends Moni capabilities.

🎯 Goal-based Planning

Save for specific goals: home, education, retirement. Visual progress tracking.

📊 Smart Portfolio

AI-suggested ETF/stock allocations based on risk profile and goals.

🔔 Smart Alerts

Proactive notifications for investment opportunities, savings milestones, and market updates.

Business Model Options

Model Pricing Pros Cons

Phase 3: Full Wealth Suite

  • • Launch goal-based investing

  • • Target: 1M wealth management users

Next Steps & Recommendations

1. Form Cross-Functional Team

Product, AI/ML, Compliance, and Business Development teams aligned on wealth management initiative.

2. Define MVP Scope

Focus on AI Savings Coach as first feature. Leverage existing Moni infrastructure.

3. Regulatory Engagement

Begin proactive discussions with SBV on AI financial advisory and robo-advisor regulations.

4. Build vs. Partner Decision

Evaluate partnership with TIFIN, Betterment, or local fund managers for AI and investment products.

5. Set Success Metrics

Target: 1M AI coaching interactions in first 6 months, 500K round-up users by month 6.

6. User Research

Conduct focus groups with high-balance TTT users to validate feature prioritization.
