SWOT Analysis — MoMo Vay Nhanh

Strengths

  • Asset-light: zero credit risk on balance sheet
  • 30M+ wallet users for distribution
  • 1-minute approval, instant disbursement
  • 64% repeat borrower rate = sticky
  • Transaction data across 60+ domains

Weaknesses

  • VietCredit 67% concentration risk
  • Approval rate declining: 73.8% → 69.0%
  • Ticket cap: 17-18M avg vs HC 250M
  • No direct lending license
  • Revenue not tracked in BQ

Opportunities

  • MBV: 80.2% approval, diversification
  • Regulatory: loan limit 4x, wallet 3x increase
  • BNPL: $2.61B growing 36.5% YoY
  • Cross-sell: 1.59M VietQR users w/o FS
  • Dormant Paylater: 995K lapsed users

Threats

  • SeaMoney: $9.2B, off-platform +300%
  • Cake: 6.2M users, income +225%
  • FE Credit going digital (13K+ POS)
  • VietCredit credit card = less MoMo dependency

MoMo's Unique Positioning

The Only Zero-Risk Distributor

MoMo is the only player distributing loans WITHOUT holding credit risk. Every competitor holds loans on balance sheet or co-risks with a bank. MoMo earns a distribution fee (est. 0.5-1% of disbursement) with zero NPL exposure. At 3.1T/month, that's ~15-31B VND/month with near-100% margin.

Key risk: If VietCredit builds its own digital distribution (credit card launch, direct app), MoMo's intermediary value weakens. Diversification via MBV and new lenders is essential.
