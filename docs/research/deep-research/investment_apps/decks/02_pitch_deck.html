Product Vision: MoMo Invest

Three pillars that turn MoMo from a payments app into a wealth-building platform.

💰

Invest-Back Rewards

Every QR payment, bill pay, or P2P transfer earns fractional fund units. Like Stash's Stock-Back, but native to MoMo's payment rails. Spend = save = invest. Daily habit loop.

🎯

Goal-Based Investing

"Tet Gift Fund", "Motorbike Fund", "Emergency Buffer". Set a goal, MoMo auto-invests. Progress visualized with beautiful animations. Behavioral psychology meets compound returns.

🤖

AI Investment Coach

Powered by Moni + your transaction data. "You spent 2M on coffee this month. Invest half of that and in 5 years you'll have..." Personalized, Vietnamese-language, always-on guidance.
