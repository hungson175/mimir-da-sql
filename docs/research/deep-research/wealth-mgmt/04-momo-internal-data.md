## Key Insights for Wealth Management Decision

1. **TTT is MoMo's Yu'e Bao** — 3.4M MAU, 11.7T AUM, users already earn 37B/month in interest. Natural bridge to investing.
2. **Money Pool is the social savings product** — 109% user growth, 223% AUM growth. Group investing could be next.
3. **15T+ monthly cashflow** through TTT but net cashout — users treat it as transit. Investment products could capture this outflow.
4. **9.35M churned funded accounts** — massive re-engagement opportunity with better returns/products.
5. **1.4M credit-active users** (Paylater) need financial coaching the most — they're borrowing to spend.
6. **InsurTech explosion** (+144% revenue) shows users are ready for more financial products.
7. **Combined FS user base**: TTT 3.4M + Paylater 1.4M + VN 148K + InsurTech 112K — significant overlap likely, but addressable market is millions.
