## 1. Paylater — The Revenue Engine

### Performance
- **MAU**: 693K (Jan 2024) → 1.44M (Dec 2025) = **+107% in 2 years**
- **GMV**: 13.9T (2024) → 23.6T (2025) = **+70%**
- **Fee Revenue**: 460B → 888B = **+93%** (fastest-growing revenue line)
- **Txns/user/month**: ~8.7 (stable) — high engagement product

### Key Dynamics
- Growth driven by BOTH user acquisition (+44% MAU YoY) AND higher utilization
- DPD fee collection started Aug 2024 — added ~30B/month by late 2025
- Credit utilization only 18% — **massive headroom** without new user acquisition
- Revenue split: 40% MoMo / 40% Bank / 20% Risk → MoMo net ~56B/month

### 2026 Outlook
- Jan-Feb 2026: MAU 1.45M, GMV run rate 28T/yr
- **Growth decelerating** from 70% to ~18% YoY — natural maturation
- Upside: dormant reactivation (87% of 1.18M dormant = lapsed, not dead)
- Risk: DPD fees growing faster than management fees — credit quality watch
