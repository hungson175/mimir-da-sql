## Executive Summary

Vay Nhanh had an **explosive 2025** — disbursing **24.18T VND** across **1.56M loans**, growing **+169% Jan→Dec**. Monthly disbursement tripled from 1.16T (Jan) to 3.13T (Dec). The product is competitive with Home Credit's estimated monthly volumes, remarkable for an asset-light platform holding zero credit risk.

The competitive landscape is recovering from a brutal 2023-2024 NPL cycle. VietCredit (MoMo's primary lender) had a record year (+136% loan growth). SeaMoney is the fastest-growing regional threat ($9.2B portfolio, 37M active credit users across SEA). Cake by VPBank is the most credible digital bank challenger (225% income growth, 6.2M users).

## SWOT for MoMo Vay Nhanh

### Strengths
- **Asset-light**: Zero credit risk on balance sheet — pure distribution fee model
- **Scale**: 30M+ wallet users, ~220-260K loan applications/month
- **Speed**: 1-minute approval, instant disbursement
- **Data advantage**: Transaction data for credit scoring across 60+ domains
- **Repeat rate**: 64% of loans are repeat borrowers

### Weaknesses
- **VietCredit concentration**: 67% of volume through one lender
- **Declining approval rate**: 73.8% → 69.0% over 6 months
- **Ticket cap**: 17-18M avg vs Home Credit up to 250M
- **No lending license**: Dependent on partner willingness
- **Revenue opaque**: MoMo's origination fee not in BQ

### Opportunities
- **MBV diversification**: 80.2% approval rate, reduces VietCredit dependency
- **Regulatory tailwind**: Loan limit 4x increase + e-wallet limit 3x increase
- **BNPL market**: $2.61B growing 36.5% YoY
- **Cross-sell**: 3.61M VietQR users, 1.59M with no FS products
- **Dormant Paylater reactivation**: 995K lapsed users

### Threats
- **SeaMoney**: $9.2B portfolio, off-platform lending growing 300%, 1.1% NPL
- **Cake**: 6.2M users, 225% income growth, first profitable digital bank in VN
- **FE Credit digital**: Market leader with 13,000+ POS going online
- **VietCredit growing independent**: Credit card launch, may reduce MoMo dependency

---

## Key Insight: MoMo's Unique Position

MoMo is the **only player** that distributes loans WITHOUT holding credit risk. Every competitor either:
- Holds the loans on balance sheet (FE Credit, Home Credit, VietCredit, MCredit)
- Funds through a banking partner with co-risk (SPayLater, Cake)

MoMo earns a distribution fee (est. 0.5-1% of disbursement) with zero NPL exposure. At 3.1T VND/month disbursement, this is a ~15-31B VND/month revenue stream with near-100% margin.

The question is defensibility: if VietCredit builds its own digital distribution (credit card launch, direct app), MoMo's intermediary position weakens.
