# Vietnam E-Wallet Competitive Analysis & Strategic Recommendations for MoMo 2026

**Research Date:** February 28, 2026  
**Prepared for:** MoMo Strategic Planning

---

## Executive Summary

Based on comprehensive market research and internal data analysis, MoMo should focus on **three strategic pillars for 2026**:

1. **SME Financial Services** - Capitalize on the $90B credit gap with data-driven lending
2. **AI-Powered Insurance Expansion** - Build on Chubb partnership to capture underserved insurance market
3. **Cross-Border Payments** - Leverage first-mover advantage in regional QR payments

**Key Data Point:** MoMo achieved first full-year profit in 2024 while CLO lending grew 732% in 2025, demonstrating successful pivot to higher-margin financial services.

## 1. Competitive Landscape Analysis

### Market Share Overview (2024-2025)

| Player | Market Share | Key Strength | 2030 Projection |
|--------|--------------|--------------|-----------------|
| **MoMo** | 46-68% | Super app ecosystem, AI capabilities, profitability | 47.3% |

| **VNPay** | 16% | Banking infrastructure | - |
| **ShopeePay** | 10-15% | E-commerce integration | - |

### Competitor Strategic Positioning

#### ZaloPay: "Open Ecosystem Platform"
- **Pivot (July 2024):** From e-wallet to "open payment ecosystem"
- **Key Differentiator:** Universal QR acceptance (bank, wallet, VNPay-QR, international)
- **International Expansion:** QR payments in 5 Asian countries (Singapore, Japan, Korea, Malaysia, Indonesia)
- **Partnership Strategy:** Cake Digital Bank (VPBank), CIMB, Visa
- **Threat Level:** MEDIUM - Strong Zalo integration but limited financial services depth

#### ViettelPay: "Financial Inclusion Champion"
- **Core Advantage:** 99% geographic coverage via Viettel infrastructure
- **Focus:** Rural, unbanked populations (72% of mobile money users in rural areas)
- **Government Integration:** National Public Service Portal, VNeID, social welfare disbursement
- **Recent Partnership:** Visa (March 2025) for SME credit solutions
- **Threat Level:** MEDIUM-HIGH - Government backing + rural moat

#### ShopeePay (Monee): "E-Commerce Lock-in"
- **Strategy:** Deep Shopee ecosystem integration (55% e-commerce market share)
- **Services:** SPayLater (BNPL), SLoan (seller financing)
- **Regional:** Part of Sea Limited's Monee fintech arm (12-14 countries)
- **Threat Level:** LOW-MEDIUM - Limited to e-commerce, declining Shopee dominance vs TikTok Shop

## 2. Internal Data Analysis: MoMo's Growth Vectors

### 2.1 CLO Lending: Explosive Growth (732% in 2025)

**Monthly Disbursement Trend:**

| Year | Month | Tickets | Disbursed (VND) | Revenue (VND) | Avg Ticket Size |
|------|-------|---------|-----------------|---------------|-----------------|
| 2025 | Dec | 4,919 | 127.7T | 4.9T | 26M |
| 2025 | Nov | 4,907 | 136.9T | 5.3T | 27.9M |
| 2025 | Oct | 6,593 | 177.3T | 6.8T | 26.9M |
| 2025 | Jun | 4,921 | 135.8T | 5.2T | 27.6M |
| 2024 | Dec | 1,717 | 48.9T | 0.9T | 28.5M |
| 2024 | Jun | 44 | 0.8T | - | 20.2M |

**Key Insights:**
- **Growth:** From 44 tickets (Jun 2024) to 6,593 tickets (Oct 2025) = **150x growth**
- **Stabilization:** Monthly run-rate ~5,000 tickets, ~130T VND disbursed
- **Revenue per ticket:** ~27M VND average, ~3.8% revenue margin
- **Partners:** Home Credit dominates (72% market share), followed by FE Credit, MCredit

**Strategic Implication:** CLO lending is MoMo's fastest-growing, highest-margin segment. Expand partner network and launch proprietary lending products.

### 2.2 P2P Revenue: W2B Monetization Success

**Monthly Trends (2025):**

**Fee-Paying User Segments (2025):**

- SME segment: 2.2M users (69%)
- Hardcore users: 1.6M users (51%)

**Strategic Implication:** W2B is MoMo's cash cow with strong SME adoption. Cross-sell financial services to 10M W2B users.

## 3. Market Trends & Regulatory Environment

### 3.1 Key Market Trends (2024-2025)

| Trend | Impact on MoMo | Opportunity |
|-------|----------------|-------------|
| **Consumer payment saturation** | 87%+ adults have bank accounts, 95%+ transactions digital | Pivot to B2B/SME services |
| **Bank app momentum** | Gen X: 30% prefer bank apps (+4pp QoQ) | Differentiate via AI + ecosystem |
| **QR code explosion** | +106.7% volume growth, 84.8% value growth | Lead VietQR cross-border initiative |
| **Biometric mandate** | Facial scan required for >10M VND transfers | Leverage AI security advantage |
| **BNPL growth** | 42% annual growth, $3.33B market by 2024 | Launch MoMo BNPL product |
| **Regulatory sandbox** | Decree 94/2025 enables Open API, P2P lending | First-mover in credit scoring innovation |

### 3.2 Regulatory Changes

**Decree 94/2025/ND-CP (April 2025):**
- Vietnam's first fintech sandbox
- Enables: Credit scoring, P2P lending, Open API testing
- Duration: 2 years (extendable)
- **MoMo Opportunity:** Pilot proprietary credit scoring using transaction data

**Biometric Verification Mandate:**
- 120.9M individual profiles verified (July 2025)
- Fraud victims dropped 57%
- **MoMo Advantage:** AI-powered fraud detection already operational

## 4. Strategic Recommendations for 2026

### 4.1 Priority 1: SME Financial Services (Revenue Potential: HIGH)

**Rationale:**
- Vietnam's SME credit gap: **$90 billion**
- MoMo already serves 500K+ SMEs via QR codes
- W2B segment: 10M users, 221,619T VND annual value
- B2B segment growing at 24.38% annually vs 15.37% overall market

**Action Items:**
1. **Launch MoMo Business Loan**
   - Target: 500K+ QR code merchants
   - Data advantage: Transaction history enables risk scoring
   - Ticket size: 50-500M VND
   - Expected volume: 10,000 loans/month by Q4 2026

2. **Expand Quick Loans for SMEs**
   - Postpaid Wallets for business cash flow
   - Integration with accounting software
   - Revenue share with software partners

3. **Soundbox + Business Intelligence**
   - Upgrade Soundbox with sales analytics
   - Inventory management integration
   - Competitive advantage vs ViettelPay/VNPay

**Success Metrics:**
- 50,000 SME loans disbursed in 2026
- 100B VND revenue from SME segment
- 20% of W2B users adopt business financial services

---

### 4.2 Priority 2: AI-Powered Insurance Expansion (Revenue Potential: HIGH)

**Rationale:**
- Chubb Life partnership (Sept 2025) proving successful
- Critical Illness Insurance+: 2-minute application, no medical exam
- Vietnam insurance penetration: <2% of GDP (vs 3-5% in ASEAN)
- Target: Young professionals, gig economy workers

**Action Items:**
1. **Expand Insurance Portfolio**
   - Health insurance (beyond critical illness)
   - Motor insurance (motorbike - 70M vehicles in Vietnam)
   - Micro-insurance (pay-per-day travel, accident)

2. **AI-Driven Personalization**
   - Risk profiling based on transaction patterns
   - Dynamic pricing for loyal users
   - Predictive cross-sell (e.g., travel insurance for flight bookers)

3. **Embedded Insurance**
   - Auto-enroll in delivery insurance for ShopeeFood/Grab orders
   - Device insurance for phone top-up users
   - Loan protection insurance for CLO borrowers

**Success Metrics:**
- 500,000 insurance policies sold in 2026
- 200B VND insurance premium volume
- Top 3 digital insurance distributor in Vietnam

---

### 4.3 Priority 3: Cross-Border Payments (Revenue Potential: MEDIUM-HIGH)

**Rationale:**
- ZaloPay launched international QR payments (Aug 2025) in 5 countries
- SBV piloting bilateral QR with Thailand, Cambodia, Laos
- Vietnam-China cross-border QR launched
- MoMo's first-mover advantage in AI/security

**Action Items:**
1. **Regional QR Payment Network**
   - Partner with NAPAS for ASEAN connectivity
   - Launch in Thailand, Singapore, Malaysia (high Vietnamese tourist destinations)
   - Target: 1M cross-border transactions by Q4 2026

2. **Remittance Services**
   - Western Union partnership expansion
   - Target: Vietnamese diaspora (5M+ overseas)
   - Market: $19.56B annual remittance inflow

3. **Tourist Payment Solutions**
   - Personal QR codes for international visitors
   - Multi-currency wallet (USD, CNY, THB, SGD)
   - Partnership with travel agencies and hotels

**Success Metrics:**
- 5M cross-border transactions in 2026
- 50B VND revenue from FX spreads and fees
- #1 Vietnamese e-wallet for international travelers

---

### 4.4 Priority 4: Defensive Strategies

**Defend Against ViettelPay:**
- **Rural penetration:** Partner with local agents, offer higher commissions
- **Government services:** Bid for national payment infrastructure contracts
- **Mobile money:** Launch USSD-based services for feature phones

**Defend Against ZaloPay:**
- **International payments:** Accelerate cross-border launch (beat ZaloPay's 5-country coverage)
- **Open ecosystem:** Launch MoMo Open API for third-party developers
- **Zalo integration:** Strengthen MoMo Mini App within Zalo (if partnership possible)

**Defend Against Bank Apps:**
- **AI differentiation:** Promote "AI Financial Assistant" positioning
- **Ecosystem lock-in:** Bundle services (payments + lending + insurance + investments)
- **User experience:** Maintain edge in app speed, reliability, UX

## 7. Conclusion

MoMo is at an inflection point. Having achieved profitability in 2024, the company must now choose between:

1. **Defensive path:** Protect consumer payment market share (saturated, low-margin)
2. **Offensive path:** Expand into higher-margin B2B/SME financial services (underserved, growing)

**Recommendation:** Pursue the offensive path with three strategic pillars:

1. **SME Financial Services** - Data-driven lending to 500K+ merchants
2. **AI-Powered Insurance** - Build on Chubb success, target 500K policies
3. **Cross-Border Payments** - Leverage AI security for regional expansion

**Expected Outcome:** 50% revenue growth in 2026, strengthened competitive moat, and sustainable profitability in the face of bank app competition.
