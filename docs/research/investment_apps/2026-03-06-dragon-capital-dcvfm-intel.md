## CRITICAL FINDING: MoMo-DCVFM Partnership Already Exists

MoMo (M-Service) signed a fund distribution partnership with DCVFM in **June 2022**. This was the first time open-ended fund certificate trading was available on a Vietnamese super-app. ([The Investor, 2022](https://theinvestor.vn/dragon-capital-kicks-off-investment-products-on-momo-e-wallet-d771.html))

**Implication:** The conversation with Dragon Capital is NOT a cold partnership pitch. It's a reactivation and expansion of an existing relationship. The LOI Thuan requires may be an amendment/expansion of existing terms.

**Open question:** Why hasn't the 2022 partnership generated significant volume? Diagnosing this gap is critical for the one-pager.
